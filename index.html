About Me

I have great project management skills, attention to detail and the ability to dive deep into work.

My industrial design practice focused on creating assistive devices for individuals with developmental disabilities and autism. After working with and interviewing this population, I found that better assistive software is an overwhelming need. I am excited to solidify the intersection between my passion for community, design and full stack software engineering.

In my career I have worked closely with individuals with developmental disabilities and LGBTQIA+ individuals looking for competent and safe healthcare. My passion for and understanding of diverse human experience makes me an excellent communicator and thoughtful developer.

My immediate goal is to find a team to learn with in a company I can get behind.
